Tips for Better Daily Planning

1

Write tasks the night before. Starting your day with a ready list saves time and reduces morning stress.

2

Keep tasks small and specific. Instead of "work on project", write "write introduction paragraph for report".

3

Do the hardest task first. Tackling the most difficult item early leaves you energized for the rest of the day.

4

Review completed tasks. Seeing what you finished gives motivation to keep going the next day.
